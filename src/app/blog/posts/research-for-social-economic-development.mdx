## Keynote Presentation Overview

On July 21st, 2006, I had the distinct honor of being the Keynote Presenter at the Egerton University Research Week. The core focus of my presentation, titled *"Research for Social Economic Development: AICAD’s Contribution towards Poverty Reduction,"* delved into the strategic initiatives undertaken by the African Institute for Capacity Development (AICAD).

## Focus Areas

- **Poverty Alleviation**: Implementing actionable research models directly targeted at uplifting local economies.
- **Capacity Building**: Highlighting AICAD's framework for enhancing skills, technology transfer, and sustainable development across East Africa.
- **Collaborative Networks**: The importance of uniting academic institutions, government bodies, and international partners to foster meaningful socio-economic changes.

This engagement served to reinforce the necessity of applied research in tackling systemic challenges in the region.
