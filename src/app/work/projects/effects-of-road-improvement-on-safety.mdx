## Overview

Published by LAMBERT Publishing and the American Journal of Civil Engineering, this extensive study investigates how significant road improvements affect traffic safety, focusing specifically on the Nairobi-Thika Superhighway in Kenya.

## Research Focus

- **Infrastructure Upgrades**: Analyzing the engineering elements that transformed the highway.
- **Traffic Safety Metrics**: Evaluating accident rates and safety indicators before and after the improvements.
- **Policy Recommendations**: Outlining how future civil engineering projects can integrate safety-first design principles.

This research provided critical feedback on one of Kenya's most massive infrastructural projects in recent decades.
