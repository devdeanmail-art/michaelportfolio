## Overview

Published in the *Journal of Geoscience and Environmental Protection*, this paper explores the innovative use of remote sensing technology to map and monitor the water quality of Lake Victoria. 

## Key Contributions

- **Remote Sensing Applications**: Utilizing satellite and aerial imagery to assess large bodies of water efficiently.
- **Environmental Protection**: Providing actionable insights to local environmental bodies to manage pollution and conservation efforts.
- **Methodological Advancements**: Combining geospatial data with traditional water sampling to establish a robust water quality model.

This 2025 publication highlights the continuous intersection of geomantic engineering and environmental sustainability in East Africa.
