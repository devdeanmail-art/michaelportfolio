## Overview

This publication, featured in the *Journal of Multidisciplinary Engineering Science Studies* and published as a book by LAMBERT Publishing, addresses the fundamental diagram of spatial analysis in urban traffic systems within Kenyan municipalities.

## Themes Explored

- **Urban Traffic Flow**: Analyzing congestion patterns and the mathematical models governing traffic movement.
- **Spatial Analysis**: Applying geographic and spatial methodologies to understand city-wide transport networks.
- **Municipal Planning**: Delivering vital data for town planners to optimize road networks and public transit routes.
