## Strategic Overview

From February 2016 to August 2019, I had the privilege of serving as the Director of Mission Support for MINUSMA, the United Nations Multidimensional Integrated Stabilization Mission in Mali. Operating from Bamako, this role involved steering one of the UN's most complex and challenging peacekeeping environments.

## Logistics and Administration

As the Director of Mission Support, I led a robust division comprising 3,000 dedicated employees. My primary responsibilities encompassed the comprehensive oversight of logistics and administration across the mission's vast operational footprint. 

Key achievements included:
- **Budget Management:** Successfully oversaw an annual operating budget of $1.2 Billion, ensuring efficient allocation of resources and strict financial accountability.
- **Supply Chain Optimization:** Streamlined logistical operations to support rapid deployment and sustained operational readiness for military and police contingents.
- **Infrastructure Development:** Championed the development and maintenance of critical mission infrastructure to ensure the safety and well-being of peacekeepers in high-risk zones.

## Lasting Impact

Our efforts in Mali fortified the mission's operational capacity, significantly contributing to the stabilization efforts in the region. The experience underscored the indispensable role of agile, well-resourced mission support in the success of global peacekeeping mandates.
