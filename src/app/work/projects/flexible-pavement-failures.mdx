## Overview

Presented at the International Civil Engineering Conference on Sustainable Development in the 21st Century, this pivotal research investigates the root causes behind the failure of flexible pavements on Kenya's primary transport arteries: the Nairobi-Thika (A02) and Nairobi-Mombasa (A109) highways.

## Core Findings

- **Material Analysis**: Investigating the longevity and stress-responses of asphalt under heavy equatorial traffic.
- **Sustainable Construction**: Proposing structural adaptations to withstand both the volume of commercial transport and regional climatic conditions.
- **Economic Impact**: Discussing the cost-benefits of robust initial construction versus long-term maintenance cycles.
