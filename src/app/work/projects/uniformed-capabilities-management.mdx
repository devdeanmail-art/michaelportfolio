## Overview

The Uniformed Capabilities Management (UCM) system is a critical component of the Umoja Extension 2 portfolio. It represents a paradigm shift in how the United Nations manages its uniformed personnel and logistics across global peacekeeping operations. By transitioning from fragmented, manual processes to an integrated digital platform, the UCM system enhances efficiency, transparency, and accountability.

## Key Features

- **Automated Strength Reporting:** Streamlines the daily reporting of military and police personnel strength across all missions.
- **Claims Processing Integration:** Accelerates the reimbursement process for Troop and Police Contributing Countries (T/PCCs).
- **Conduct and Discipline Tracking:** Provides robust modules for recording and managing personnel records.
- **Operational Readiness Assessment:** Facilitates real-time monitoring of mission readiness and equipment availability.

## Impact

The implementation of the UCM system has significantly reduced the administrative burden on field missions and UN Headquarters. It has improved the accuracy of data used for strategic decision-making and ensured that T/PCCs are reimbursed accurately and on time, strengthening the UN's partnerships with member states.

As Director of the Uniformed Capability Support Division (UCSD), my role involves overseeing the continued rollout, enhancement, and strategic alignment of the UCM system with the UN's broader operational goals.
